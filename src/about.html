Our Story

How GigLega came to be

GigLega was born from a simple observation — India has immense talent, but finding reliable short-term work or a trustworthy local helper is still frustratingly hard. Especially for students, bachelors, and young professionals living in cities like Gurugram.

We wanted to build something genuinely local. A platform where posting a gig takes under 2 minutes, and finding a worker nearby takes even less. No middlemen, no bloated processes — just fast, fair, and direct connections.

GigLega started in Gurugram in early 2026 as a Beta. Every feature has been shaped by real feedback from real people in this city.

Mission & Values

What we stand for

Everything we build is guided by a few core principles that keep us honest and useful.

📍

Hyperlocal First

We believe the best opportunities are closest to home. GigLega prioritizes your neighborhood before anything else.

⚖️

Fair for Everyone

Workers deserve fair pay and posters deserve fair prices. Our fee structure is transparent — 6% only when work is done.

🔒

Safety & Trust

Every interaction on GigLega is designed to keep both sides safe — from secure payments to in-platform chat.

⚡

Speed Matters

Urgent gigs need fast workers. We've designed everything to move quickly — post in 2 minutes, get matched in minutes.

🌱

Community Growth

GigLega grows when our users grow. We invest in features that help workers build reputation and earn more over time.

🤝

Zero Exploitation

No race to the bottom on pricing. We enforce minimum wage floors and prohibit unfair gig conditions on our platform.

How It Works

Simple steps, real results

Whether you're looking for work or looking to hire — the process is the same: fast, simple, and transparent.

📝

Create Account

Sign up free in under 30 seconds. Add your skills and preferred work area.

🔍

Browse Gigs

Filter by category, location, and pay rate. See real-time available gigs near you.

✅

Accept & Work

First-come-first-served. Accept a gig, chat with the poster, and get to work.

💰

Get Paid

Receive payment after completion — cash, UPI, or GigLega wallet. You keep 94%.

➕

Post a Gig

Describe the task, set the pay rate and location. It takes under 2 minutes.

👀

Worker Accepts

Nearby workers see your gig instantly. One accepts and it's locked in for them.

💬

Chat & Coordinate

Use the built-in chat to share details, address, and timing with your worker.

🎯

Mark Complete

Once done, mark the gig complete. Payment releases and both parties rate each other.

Our Journey

From idea to platform

Late 2025
The Idea
Frustrated by how hard it was to find reliable part-time help in Gurugram, the GigLega concept was born — a local-first gig platform with no unnecessary complexity.
Early 2026
Beta Launch
GigLega Beta went live at giglega.vercel.app. Core features launched: gig posting, browsing, in-app chat, worker and poster dashboards, and secure payment options.
Mid 2026 — Upcoming
Real Payments + Mobile App
Razorpay / UPI integration, GigLega Android app, push notifications, and identity verification for trusted worker badges.
Late 2026 — Planned
Expanding Beyond Gurugram
Rolling out to Delhi, Noida, and Faridabad. Enterprise tier for businesses with recurring gig needs. Subscription plans for power users.
