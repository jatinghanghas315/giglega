💎 Hamare Core Values

Yeh principles GigLega ke har decision ko guide karte hain.

🤝
Trust First
Har worker aur poster ka trust earn karna hamare liye sabse pehle aata hai. Verified profiles, honest reviews, transparent fees.
⚡
Speed Matters
Urgent kaam ke liye urgent platform chahiye. GigLega pe gig post karo aur ghanton mein response milta hai — days mein nahi.
💰
Fair Economy
Sirf 6% platform fee — workers ko zyada milna chahiye. Koi hidden charges, koi subscription traps. Jo dikhe wahi lage.
📍
Local Impact
Gurugram ke local economy ko strong karna haara mission hai. Har gig ek local connection hai, ek local income hai.
🔒
Safe Platform
Personal number share karne ki zaroorat nahi. In-app chat, safety guidelines, aur 24/7 report system — sab available hai.
🌱
Always Improving
v1.1 abhi launch hua. Har version mein naye features, better UX, aur community feedback ka implementation hoga.

⚙️ Platform Kaise Kaam Karta Hai

Worker aur Poster dono ke liye step-by-step simple process.

1

Free mein Register karo

Signup pe naam, email ya phone, aur apna city daalo. Skills select karo — 2 minute mein profile ready.

2

Nearby Gigs Browse karo

Category, budget, aur type se filter karo. Apne area ke sabse relevant gigs dekho.

3

Gig Accept karo

Ek click mein gig accept karo. Price breakdown clearly dikhai deta hai — fee kati hogi, baaki tumhara.

4

Chat → Kaam Karo → Kamao

In-app chat mein poster se details lo. Kaam karo. Payment wallet mein — same day. Simple.

1

Account banao

Free signup — "Hire Help" choose karo. Poster dashboard instantly ready ho jaata hai.

2

Gig Post karo

Title, description, category, budget, location — sab fill karo. 3 minute max. Gig turant live ho jaata hai.

3

Worker Milta Hai

Nearby verified workers tumhara gig dekhenge aur accept karenge. Notification milega turant.

4

Chat, Confirm, Done!

Worker se in-app chat mein timing aur address confirm karo. Kaam ho jae toh review do.

📅 Our Journey

GigLega ki growth story — shuru se abhi tak.

Early 2025
💡 Idea Ka Janam
Gurugram ke bachelors aur students ke liye ek hyperlocal gig platform ka concept aaya — jahan kaam dhoondhna aur kaam dena dono aasaan ho.
Mid 2025
🛠 v1.0 Build
Pehla version build kiya — basic gig posting, browsing, aur user auth. localStorage-based MVP launched for testing.
Late 2025
🧪 Beta Testing
First real users onboard kiye. Feedback liya — bugs fix kiye, UI improve kiya, aur chat feature add kiya.
March 2026
🚀 v1.1 Launch
Complete rebuild — bug-free, fully responsive, dark mode, notifications, wallet system, aur clean architecture ke saath v1.1 live!
Coming Soon
📱 Mobile App + v2.0
Android app, real payment gateway integration, GPS-based gig matching, aur NCR-wide expansion — yeh sab roadmap mein hai.
