Help Center & FAQ

GigLega ke baare mein

GigLega kya hai?
GigLega ek hyperlocal gig platform hai jo Gurugram ke logo ko local aur remote kaam dhoondhne mein madad karta hai. Yahaan aap gig work, freelance projects, aur part-time jobs dhoondh sakte hain.
Kya GigLega use karna free hai?
Haan! GigLega Beta version bilkul free hai. Aap bina kisi charges ke kaam dhoondh sakte hain aur post kar sakte hain.

Account & Registration

Account kaise banaye?
'Free Mein Judo!' button pe click karein, apni details fill karein (naam, email, phone, location), aur account type select karein (Freelancer, Gig Worker, ya Client).
Login kaise karein?
'Log In' button pe click karein aur apna email aur password enter karein. Google login option bhi available hai.

Gig Dhoondhna

Gigs kaise dhoondhein?
'Browse Gigs' pe jaayein, apni category select karein (Home Services, Digital Marketing, etc.), aur location/price range ke hisaab se filter lagayein.
Hyperlocal gigs kya hain?
Hyperlocal gigs wo kaam hain jo aapke nearby area mein hain - jaise cleaning, delivery, pet care, etc. Ye gigs orange badge ke saath dikhti hain.

Gig Post Karna

Gig kaise post karein?
'Post a Gig' pe click karein, apne kaam ki details bh arein (title, description, budget, location), aur submit karein. Approved hone ke baad aapki gig live ho jayegi.
Kya fixed aur hourly dono rate set kar sakte hain?
Haan! Aap 'Fixed' ya 'Hourly' choose kar sakte hain. Hourly gigs ke liye Gurugram ke local wage rates automatically suggest ho jate hain.

Payment & Safety

Payment kaise hota hai?
Abhi Beta version mein, payment direct client aur worker ke beech hota hai. Future mein integrated payment system aayega.
Kya GigLega safe hai?
Haan! Hum rating system aur verification process use karte hain. Kisi bhi suspicious activity ko report karein.
