Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

GigLega aapki privacy ko seriously leta hai. Ye policy explain karti hai ki hum aapki personal information ko kaise collect, use, aur protect karte hain.

2. Information Hum Collect Karte Hain

Account Information:

  • Naam, email address, phone number
  • Location (Gurugram area)
  • User role (Freelancer, Gig Worker, Client)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Gig Information:

  • Gig descriptions aur details
  • Pricing aur payment information
  • Work history aur reviews

Usage Data:

  • Device information (browser, OS)
  • IP address aur location data
  • Platform usage patterns
  • Cookies aur similar technologies

3. Information Ka Use Kaise Karte Hain

  • Platform services provide karne ke liye
  • User profiles aur gig listings manage karne ke liye
  • Users ko match karne ke liye (clients aur workers)
  • Platform improve karne aur analytics ke liye
  • Customer support provide karne ke liye
  • Fraud prevention aur security ke liye

4. Information Sharing

GigLega aapki personal information third parties ke saath share nahi karta, except:

  • Other Users: Aapka public profile (naam, photo, reviews) dusre users ko dikhta hai
  • Legal Requirements: Agar law ya court order require kare
  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication aur database)
  • Business Transfer: Agar company acquire hoti hai

5. Data Security

Hum industry-standard security measures use karte hain:

  • Firebase Authentication se secure login
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security audits

Lekin remember karein: Internet par koi bhi transmission 100% secure nahi hai.

6. Your Rights

Aapko ye rights hain:

  • Access: Apni data dekhne ka right
  • Correction: Galat information correct karne ka right
  • Deletion: Account delete karne ka right
  • Data Portability: Apni data download karne ka right
  • Opt-out: Marketing communications se opt-out karne ka right

7. Cookies & Tracking

Hum cookies use karte hain:

  • User login sessions maintain karne ke liye
  • Platform preferences save karne ke liye
  • Analytics aur performance tracking ke liye

Aap browser settings mein cookies disable kar sakte hain.

8. Children's Privacy

GigLega 18 saal se kam age ke users ke liye nahi hai. Hum knowingly 18 se kam age ke users ki information collect nahi karte.

9. Data Retention

Hum aapki information tab tak retain karte hain jab tak:

  • Aapka account active hai
  • Services provide karne ke liye zaruri hai
  • Legal requirements ke liye zaruri hai

Account delete karne par, aapki data 30 days mein permanently delete ho jayegi.

10. Third-Party Links

Platform par dusri websites ke links ho sakte hain. Hum un websites ki privacy policies ke liye responsible nahi hain.

11. Changes to This Policy

Hum is privacy policy ko kabhi bhi update kar sakte hain. Important changes ki notification email ya platform pe di jayegi.

12. International Users

GigLega primarily India (Gurugram) ke liye hai. Aapki data India mein store hoti hai aur Indian privacy laws apply hote hain.
