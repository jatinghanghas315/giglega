Section 01
Overview — Simple Language Mein

GigLega ("hum", "platform") ek online gig marketplace hai jo workers aur posters ko connect karta hai — primarily Gurugram NCR aur surrounding areas mein. Yeh Privacy Policy explain karta hai ki jab tum haara platform use karte ho toh hum kaunsa data collect karte hain, kyun collect karte hain, aur usse kaise protect karte hain.

💡 TLDR Version: Hum sirf wahi data lete hain jo platform chalane ke liye chahiye. Kabhi sell nahi karte. Aap kabhi bhi delete kar sakte ho. Koi surprise nahi.

Yeh Policy Kab Apply Hoti Hai

Yeh policy apply hoti hai jab tum GigLega website (giglega.in), mobile app (coming soon), ya kisi bhi associated service use karte ho. Third-party websites ya services pe apply nahi hoti — unki apni policies hoti hain.

Data Controller

GigLega is operated by GigLega Technologies, Gurugram, Haryana, India — 122001. Haara Data Protection Officer (DPO) se contact karo: privacy@giglega.in

Section 07
Your Rights — DPDP Act 2023

India ke Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 ke under, aapke paas following rights hain. Hum inhe seriously lete hain.

👁️
Right to Access
Jaano ki hum kaunsa data rakhte hain tumhare baare mein. Data summary request karo anytime.
✏️
Right to Correct
Galat ya outdated information correct karo — profile settings se ya DPO ko email karo.
🗑️
Right to Erasure
Apna account aur data permanently delete karo. 30 din mein processed — legally required records exclude karke.
📤
Right to Data Portability
Apna data machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) mein download karo. Settings → Privacy → Download My Data.
🚫
Right to Object
Data processing ke kuch uses ke against object karo — jaise analytics ya profiling. DPO ko email karo.
📋
Right to Withdraw Consent
Kisi bhi consent (jaise marketing notifications) ko anytime withdraw karo — settings ya email se.
😤
Right to Grievance
Hamara response satisfactory nahi laga? DPDP Board of India ko complaint kar sakte ho.
🔒
Right to Restriction
Processing restrict karo specific situations mein — jab accuracy dispute ho ya objection pending ho.

Rights Exercise Kaise Karein

Zyaatar rights Settings page se directly exercise kar sakte ho. Specific requests ke liye:

  • 📧 Email: privacy@giglega.in
  • 📝 Subject line: "Privacy Rights Request — [Your Request Type]"
  • ⏱️ Response time: 30 days (complex cases mein 60 days)
  • 🆓 Free of charge — unless requests are clearly unfounded or excessive
💡 Identity Verification: Rights requests process karne ke liye hum tumhari identity verify karenge — registered phone/email se OTP. Impersonation prevent karne ke liye.

Grievance Escalation

Agar haara response 30 din mein nahi aaya ya satisfactory nahi laga, tum Data Protection Board of India ke paas complaint kar sakte ho — once operational under DPDP Act 2023.

Section 08
Children's Privacy
🚫 GigLega strictly 18 years aur usse upar ke users ke liye hai. 18 se kam age ke kisi bhi person ka account allowed nahi hai.

DPDP Act 2023 Section 9 ke under, hum 18 se kam age ke users ka data knowingly collect nahi karte. Agar hamare paas yeh believe karne ka reason ho ki koi minor ne account create kiya hai toh hum:

  • Account turant suspend karenge
  • Collected data delete karenge
  • Parent/guardian ko (agar identifiable ho) notify karenge

Agar tumhe kisi minor ka account dikhta hai platform pe, turant report karo safety@giglega.in pe.
