1. 👋 Introduction

GigLega ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data when you use the GigLega platform at giglega.vercel.app.

By using GigLega, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

🛡️ During Beta, all user data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). No data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly interact with external services (e.g., WhatsApp).

3. 🎯 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your GigLega account.
  • Match Workers with Posters based on location and category.
  • Facilitate communication between Posters and Workers.
  • Process and track gig payments and platform fees.
  • Improve platform features and fix bugs.
  • Send important platform notifications (no spam).
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent fraud.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

4. 💾 Data Storage

Current (Beta Phase): All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data exists only on your device and browser.
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your GigLega data.
  • Data is not synced across devices.

Future (Post-Beta): Data will be migrated to Firebase (Google Cloud) servers with encryption at rest and in transit. Servers will be located in Mumbai, India (asia-south1 region) for compliance with Indian data laws.

🔒 Profile photos are stored as base64 strings in localStorage. They are not uploaded to any server during Beta.
5. 🤝 Data Sharing

We share limited data only in these situations:

  • Between users: Your name, phone number, and gig details are shared with the other party after a gig is accepted.
  • WhatsApp: If you click "Message on WhatsApp", you are redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies.
  • OpenStreetMap (Nominatim): If you use "Fetch Location", your GPS coordinates are sent to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim API for reverse-geocoding. No account data is shared.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by Indian law or court order.

We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third-party marketing services.

6. ✊ Your Rights

You have the following rights over your data:

  • Access: View all your data in your Profile page.
  • Edit: Update your information at any time from Profile settings.
  • Delete: Delete your account from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account.
  • Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the platform and delete your account at any time.

For any data-related requests, email us at Giglega.official@gmail.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

7. 🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

GigLega currently uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store:

  • Your login session data.
  • Your profile and preferences.
  • Gig listings and chat history.
  • Dark mode preference.

No third-party tracking cookies are used on GigLega during Beta. Post-launch, analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) may be added with appropriate consent mechanisms.

8. 👶 Children's Privacy

GigLega is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at Giglega.official@gmail.com and we will remove the account.
