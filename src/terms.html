5. 💰 Payments & Fees

GigLega charges a 6% Support Fee on every completed gig, deducted from the worker's earnings.

  • Pay After Completion: Poster pays worker directly after completion. GigLega support fee applies.
  • Secure Wallet Payment: Poster's payment is held in GigLega's secure wallet until the gig is marked complete, then released to the worker minus the support fee.
  • Cash Payment: Worker is paid in cash by the poster. The GigLega support fee (6%) is collected digitally from the poster after completion.

Prices are set by Posters within GigLega's suggested and maximum ranges per category. Minimum gig price is ₹150/hr for basic tasks and ₹300/hr for skilled work.

💡 During Beta, all payments are simulated. No real money is transferred. Real payment integration (Razorpay/UPI) will be activated in the full launch.
