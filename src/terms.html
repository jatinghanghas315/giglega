For Gig Posters

Gig post karke tum represent karte ho ki:

  • Kaam real hai aur described as accurately as possible
  • Budget genuine hai — bait-and-switch nahi
  • Agreed payment accepted worker ko milegi bina undue deduction ke
  • Working conditions safe aur legal hain

For Workers

Gig accept karke tum agree karte ho ki:

  • Described kaam professionally complete karoge
  • Agreed time pe show up karoge ya advance mein cancel karoge
  • Platform ke through payment loge — outside nahi
  • Koi misrepresentation nahi karoge apni skills ya experience ki
  • Poster ki reasonable instructions follow karoge (job scope ke andar)
💡 Independent Contractor: GigLega pe workers independent contractors hain — employees nahi. GigLega employer nahi hai. Tax, PF, ESI compliance worker ki apni zimmedari hai (applicable hone pe).

Section 06
Payments & Fees
6%
Standard Platform Fee
Worker ki earnings se automatically deducted hoti hai gig complete hone ke baad. Posters ke liye gig posting free hai. Business plan pe 4%, Enterprise pe negotiated rate.

Payment Flow

  1. Poster gig post karta hai with budget
  2. Worker apply karta hai → Poster accept karta hai
  3. Kaam complete hota hai
  4. Poster "Mark Complete" karta hai
  5. Platform fee (6%) automatically deducted
  6. Net amount worker ke wallet mein credited
  7. Worker apni bank account ya UPI pe withdraw karta hai

Mandatory Platform Payment

🚫 Off-Platform Payments Prohibited: GigLega connections ke through mile workers/posters ke beech platform ke bahar cash, UPI, bank transfer, ya koi bhi payment — Terms violation hai. GigLega off-platform transactions ke liye koi protection ya dispute resolution provide nahi kar sakta.

Payment Obligations

ObligationWhoDetails
Pay agreed amount Poster Full agreed budget — no unilateral deduction
Complete work Worker As described before receiving full payment
Mark complete Poster Within 24h of gig completion — else auto-released after 72h
Dispute window Both 48 hours after gig completion to raise disputes

Auto-Release Policy

Agar poster 72 ghante mein "Mark Complete" nahi karta aur koi dispute nahi raise karta, payment automatically worker ke wallet mein release ho jaati hai. Yeh workers ko protect karta hai undue payment delays se.

GST & Taxes

Platform fee pe 18% GST applicable hai (SAC 998314). Workers apni gig income ke liye khud income tax compliance maintain karenge. GigLega TDS deduct nahi karta currently — threshold ke hisaab se future mein change ho sakta hai.

Withdrawal

Minimum withdrawal: ₹200. Processing time: 24–48 hours (working days). UPI instant transfers available. Bank transfers NEFT/IMPS via. Fraudulent withdrawal attempts pe legal action possible hai.

Platform as Intermediary

GigLega ek intermediary platform hai — IT Act 2000 Section 79 ke under. Platform pe listed gigs, workers ya posters ke actions ke liye GigLega directly liable nahi hai jab tak hum reasonably act karte rahe.

What GigLega Is NOT Responsible For

  • Quality of work done by workers
  • Accuracy of gig descriptions by posters
  • Off-platform transactions ya agreements
  • Physical safety incidents (tum responsible ho safe gig practices ke liye)
  • Third-party service failures (payment processors, internet, etc.)
  • Force majeure events — natural disasters, government actions, etc.
  • Indirect, consequential ya punitive damages

Maximum Liability Cap

Agar kisi bhi circumstance mein GigLega liable ho, maximum liability last 3 months mein tumhare account pe GigLega ko paid platform fees tak limited hai — ya ₹10,000 — whichever is lower.

⚠️ Important: Yeh limitation applicable hai maximum extent allowed by Indian law ke andar. Consumer protection rights ke under kuch rights override nahi ho sakte — Indian Consumer Protection Act 2019 applicable hai.

Indemnification

Tum agree karte ho GigLega ko indemnify karne ke liye (har ek claim, loss, damage, legal fees se protect karne ke liye) agar:

  • Tumne in Terms violate kiye
  • Tumne koi third party ke rights violate kiye

  • Tumne false ya misleading information provide ki

"As Is" Service

GigLega platform "as is" aur "as available" basis pe provide kiya jaata hai. Hum 100% uptime, error-free operation, ya specific results guarantee nahi karte — though hum best effort basis pe reliable service maintain karte hain.

Section 10
Account Termination

You Can Leave Anytime

Tum kabhi bhi apna account deactivate ya delete kar sakte ho — Dashboard → Settings → Account. Account delete karne pe:

  • Personal data 30 days mein remove ho jaayega
  • Pending earnings withdraw kar lo pehle
  • Active gigs complete karo ya cancel karo
  • Legally required records (transaction history) 7 years retain honge

GigLega Can Terminate

GigLega account terminate ya suspend kar sakta hai agar:

  • Terms of Service violation ho
  • Fraudulent ya illegal activity detect ho
  • Community safety ke liye necessary ho
  • Prolonged inactivity (12+ months) — with advance notice
  • Legal requirement ya court order ho
ℹ️ Notice: Minor violations pe pehle warning milegi — except serious violations (fraud, harassment, illegal activity) jahan immediate termination hogi.

Effect of Termination

Account terminate hone pe:

  • Platform access immediately blocked
  • Active gigs cancelled — workers notified
  • Pending earnings: cleared payments released, disputed ones held
  • Reviews aur ratings publicly visible reh sakte hain (anonymised)

Appeal Process

Account wrongly terminate hua lage toh 30 days ke andar appeal karo: appeals@giglega.in. Subject: "Account Appeal — [Phone/Email]". 7 business days mein response milega. Permanent bans rarely reversed hain — serious evidence required.
