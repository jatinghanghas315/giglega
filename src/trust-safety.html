Trust & Safety

GigLega par aapki safety hamari priority hai. Ye guidelines follow karke aap safe rehenge.

⚠️ Safety Tips

⚡ Never share personal banking details on the platform
Payments direct handle karein, platform par account numbers share na karein.

1. Safe Communication

  • Pehle platform par hi communicate karein
  • Suspicious messages ko report karein
  • Personal contact details cautiously share karein
  • Video call se identity verify karein (especially remote gigs)

2. Payment Safety

  • Advance payment sirf trusted users ko dein
  • Milestones banayein bade projects ke liye
  • Payment receipt zaroor lein
  • Kaam complete hone ke baad hi full payment karein
  • UPI/bank transfer use karein (cash risky hai)

3. Meeting Safety (Hyperlocal Gigs)

  • Pehli meeting public place mein karein
  • Kisi ko apni location batayein
  • Trust your instincts - agar uncomfortable feel ho, cancel karein
  • ID proof verify karein

4. Verify Users

  • Profile ratings aur reviews check karein
  • Verified badge wale users prefer karein
  • New users ke saath extra careful rahein
  • Too-good-to-be-true deals se bachein

5. Red Flags 🚩

  • Bahut jaldi decision lene ki pressure
  • Unrealistic promises ya pricing
  • Platform ke bahar payment demands
  • Vague gig descriptions
  • No reviews/ratings
  • Refusing video verification

6. Report Issues

Agar kuch suspicious lagta hai, turant report karein:

  • Harassment ya inappropriate behavior
  • Scams ya fraud attempts
  • Fake profiles
  • Payment disputes

Report Email: Giglega.official@gmail.com
WhatsApp: 9319635257

7. For Women Safety

  • Solo gigs ke liye daylight hours prefer karein
  • Trusted friend/family ko location share karein
  • Emergency contacts ready rakhein
  • Uncomfortable situation mein immediately leave karein

8. COVID-19 Safety

  • Mask pehen kar milein
  • Social distancing maintain karein
  • Bimar hone par gig cancel karein

9. Data Privacy

  • Strong passwords use karein
  • 2-factor authentication enable karein
  • Shared devices par logout karein
  • Suspicious links par click na karein

10. Platform Actions

GigLega kya karta hai safety ke liye:

  • User verification system
  • Rating & review system
  • Report & block features
  • 24/7 support team
  • Suspicious activity monitoring
📢 Remember: Agar kuch galat lagta hai, wo galat hi hai. Your safety > Any gig. Immediately contact support.
