🚩 Watch Out

Scam Red Flags — Inse Bacho

Yeh signs dikhein toh turant gig se hat jao aur report karo.

⚠️

Inme se koi bhi dikhne pe platform pe report karo — immediately.

GigLega pe zero tolerance policy hai fraud, harassment aur scam ke liye. Ek report se community safe rehti hai.

1
Advance Payment Demand
Koi bhi poster ya worker gig se pehle advance maange — bahar UPI, Paytm ya cash — yeh 100% scam hai.
2
Too Good to Be True Budget
₹500 kaam ke liye ₹10,000 offer — bait hai. Real gigs market rate pe hoti hain. Unrealistic budgets = trap.
3
Off-Platform Communication Push
"WhatsApp pe aao, wahan baat karte hain" — yeh platform protection hataane ki koshish hai. Sabhi baat in-app karo.
4
Aadhaar / Bank Details Maangna
Koi bhi legitimate poster ya worker tumhara Aadhaar number, bank account ya OTP kabhi nahi maangta gig ke liye.
5
New Profile + Urgency + High Pay
Naya account + "aaj hi karo" urgency + bahut high pay — yeh combination almost always fraudulent hai.
6
Suspicious Links / App Downloads
Chat mein koi link bheje "payment ke liye" ya koi app install karne ko kahe — click mat karo, report karo.
7
Harassment Ya Threatening
Koi bhi threatening language, personal information leak karne ki threat, ya harassment — turant report aur block karo.
8
Repeated Scope Changes
Gig accept ke baad poster baar baar scope badhaata rahe ya agreed payment se kam de — yeh unfair practice hai.

📄 Policies

Platform Policies — Summary

Full legal docs nahi chahiye? Yeh simple summaries hain key policies ki.

Respect & Professionalism

Har user — worker ya poster — se respectful behavior expected hai. Abusive language, personal attacks ya discrimination (based on gender, religion, caste) strictly prohibited hai.

Honest Listings

Gig descriptions accurate honi chahiye. Misleading titles, fake budgets ya bait-and-switch tactics platform violation hai aur account suspension le sakta hai.

Genuine Reviews

Reviews honest honi chahiye — fake positive ya negative reviews dono violations hain. Incentivised reviews (paise dekar) strictly banned hain.

Communication Standards

  • In-app chat professional rehni chahiye
  • Unsolicited spam messages banned hain
  • Off-platform contact push karna violation hai
Last updated: March 2026 — GigLega v1.1

Platform Fee

GigLega 6% platform fee leta hai worker ki earnings se. Business plan users ke liye 4%, Enterprise ke liye custom negotiated rate. Koi hidden fees nahi.

Payment Flow

Poster budget post karta hai → Worker accept karta hai → Kaam complete hota hai → Poster confirm karta hai → Platform fee deduct hoti hai → Worker ko net amount milta hai.

Disputes

Koi bhi payment dispute 48 ghante ke andar report karo. Platform ke bahar kiye gaye transactions ke liye GigLega responsible nahi hai.

Refunds

  • Worker kaam shuru karne se pehle cancel kare → Full refund
  • Kaam aadha complete ho → Partial refund (case-by-case)
  • Kaam complete ho jaaye → Refund possible nahi
Last updated: March 2026 — GigLega v1.1

Data We Collect

Name, phone/email, location (city level), gig history aur ratings. No Aadhaar, no bank details stored on platform directly.

How We Use It

  • Gig matching aur recommendations
  • Safety verification aur fraud prevention
  • Platform improvement analytics (anonymised)

What We Don't Do

Hum kabhi tumhara data sell nahi karte, third-party advertisers ko share nahi karte. Government requests ke alawa data share nahi hota legally without consent.

Your Rights

Apna data delete karne ka request karo — settings ya email se. Account delete karne pe sab personal data 30 days mein permanently remove hota hai.

Last updated: March 2026 — GigLega v1.1

Immediate Permanent Ban

  • Fraud ya scam attempt
  • Physical threats ya violence
  • Identity theft ya impersonation
  • Child safety violations
  • Multiple fake accounts

Warning → Suspension

  • Harassment (repeated)
  • Fake reviews manipulation
  • Off-platform payment pushing
  • Misleading gig descriptions

Appeal Process

Account ban hone pe appeal karo appeals@giglega.in pe — 7 business days mein response milega. Permanent bans rarely reversed hain.
