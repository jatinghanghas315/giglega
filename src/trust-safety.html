Payment Safety

How secure payments work

GigLega's Secure Wallet acts like an escrow — the poster's payment is held safely and only released to the worker when the gig is marked complete by both parties.

✓

Pay only after work is done

Posters are never charged until they confirm the gig was completed to satisfaction.

✓

Workers are guaranteed payment

Once a gig is marked complete, funds release automatically. No chasing, no delays.

✓

Multiple payment options

Cash, UPI, or GigLega Secure Wallet — you choose what's most comfortable.

✓

Dispute-protected transactions

If there's a disagreement, funds stay frozen until our team resolves the issue fairly.

Secure Wallet Flow

1

Poster deposits payment

When a gig is accepted, the poster deposits the agreed amount into GigLega's secure wallet.

2

Funds held safely

Money sits in escrow — neither party can access it until the gig is confirmed complete.

3

Gig marked complete

Poster confirms the work is done. Both sides get to rate each other at this point.

4

Payment released to worker

Funds are instantly released to the worker's wallet, minus the 6% GigLega fee.

Safe Usage

Do's and Don'ts on GigLega

Follow these simple guidelines to keep yourself and others safe on the platform.

✅ Do This
  • ✓Always use GigLega's in-app chat for all communication before meeting in person.
  • ✓Meet for the first time in a public, visible location for in-person gigs.
  • ✓Use GigLega Secure Wallet for payment — it protects both sides.
  • ✓Rate honestly after every gig — your reviews protect the community.
  • ✓Report suspicious profiles or messages immediately using the Report button.
  • ✓Read gig details carefully before accepting or posting — be specific about what's needed.
❌ Don't Do This
  • ✗Never pay or accept payment outside GigLega for a gig posted on the platform.
  • ✗Don't share your Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details with any user directly.
  • ✗Don't agree to work before the gig is formally accepted through the platform.
  • ✗Never ignore red flags — pressure to pay upfront, vague job details, or unusually high pay.
  • ✗Don't create fake reviews or manipulate ratings — it results in permanent account removal.
  • ✗Don't share your GigLega login credentials with anyone, including support staff.
