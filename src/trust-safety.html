💼 Workers Ke Liye Safety Tips

Kaam dhundte waqt inhe follow karo — safe rahoge.

✅ Yeh Zaroor Karo

  • Profile mein real name aur phone dalo — clients trust karte hain
  • Apply karne se pehle gig description carefully padho
  • Pehli baar milne ka plan ho toh public jagah chunna
  • Payment terms — amount, timing, mode — WhatsApp pe confirm karo
  • Kaam shuru karne se pehle advance ya partial payment lo agar bada kaam ho
  • Client ke saath saari baat WhatsApp par karo — record rehega
  • Koi bhi suspicious activity immediately GigLega ko report karo

🚫 Yeh Mat Karo

  • Kisi ko bhi advance fees mat do — GigLega kabhi nahi maangta
  • Kisi ka OTP, UPI PIN ya bank details mat batao
  • Unknown location par akele mat jao — kisi ko batao kahan ja rahe ho
  • Gig mein di gayi info ke alawa extra personal info mat share karo
  • Kaam shuru mat karo jab tak payment terms clear na ho
  • Pressure mein aake koi agreement sign mat karo bina padhe
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers par blindly trust mat karo

🏢 Clients Ke Liye Safety Tips

Workers hire karte waqt inhe dhyan mein rakho.

✅ Best Practices

  • Gig description mein exact kaam clearly likho — vague listings kam responses dete hain
  • Realistic budget set karo — unrealistic offers credibility harm karte hain
  • Worker se milne se pehle phone par baat karo pehle
  • References ya past work examples maango agar bada project ho
  • Payment schedule pehle se agree karo — milestone-based better hai
  • Worker ko clearly batao kaam kab tak chahiye
  • Kaam complete hone par time par payment karo

⚠️ Avoid These Mistakes

  • Illegal ya unethical kaam ke liye platform mat use karo
  • Workers se bina payment ke "trial work" mat karwao
  • Personal financial information workers ke saath share mat karo
  • Misleading gig titles mat use karo — clickbait descriptions banned hain
  • Hire karne ke baad unexpectedly scope mat badhao bina extra pay ke
  • Workers ko harass ya threaten mat karo
  • Spam gigs post mat karo — account ban ho sakta hai

🚩 Red Flags — Inse Bacho

Yeh cheezein dikhein toh immediate action lo — report karo ya avoid karo.

💰
Advance Fee Maangna Koi bhi genuine client ya platform register karne, training ya "security deposit" ke liye fee nahi maangta.
🤑
Unrealistic Pay ₹5000/day for basic tasks, ₹50,000 monthly for simple delivery — too good to be true hai toh fake hai.
📵
Platform Se Bahar Le Jaana "Telegram pe aao", "Yeh app download karo" — genuine clients platform par hi communicate karte hain.
🏦
Bank Details Maangna Interview se pehle account number, IFSC ya UPI ID maangna — never share karo.
🔏
OTP ya Password Maangna Koi bhi legitimate employer kabhi OTP, PIN ya password nahi maangta — yeh pure fraud hai.
⏰
Urgency Pressure "Aaj hi decide karo", "Last chance hai" — fake urgency create karna fraud ka classic sign hai.
📝
Vague Job Description No clear tasks, no clear pay, no clear location — agar kuch bhi clear nahi toh apply mat karo.
🧑‍💻
Fake Identity Profile photo stolen, company name unknown ya unverifiable, contact info fake — trust mat karo.

✅ Green Flags — Trustworthy Signs

Yeh cheezein dikhein toh proceed karna safe hai.

📋
Clear Job Description Exact tasks, clear budget, defined timeline — professional client ki pehchaan.
📞
Phone Par Baat Karna Real clients baat karne mein comfortable hote hain — text-only avoid karte hain fraudsters.
💼
Local Business / Real Address Verifiable Gurugram address ya local business name — legitimacy ka sign hai.
🤝
Interview Ya Meeting In-person ya video call interview — genuine employers candidates meet karna chahte hain.
💸
Market Rate Pay Realistic salary — na bahut zyada na bahut kam. Market standard ke around.
📑
Written Agreement Payment terms WhatsApp ya document par — professional hai aur evidence bhi.

🤝 Pehli Baar Milne Ke Tips

Client ya worker se physically milne ka plan ho toh yeh steps follow karo.

1

Kisi Ko Batao Kahan Ja Rahe Ho

Family member ya friend ko exact location, time aur client/worker ka naam aur number batao. Simple safety habit.

2

Public Jagah Chunna

Pehli meeting kisi cafe, mall ya public office mein karo — unknown person ke ghar ya isolated jagah mat jao pehli baar.

3

Phone Par Pehle Baat Karo

In-person jaane se pehle ek short phone call karo — genuineness verify karo. Real employer call se nervous nahi hota.

4

Payment Terms Pehle Confirm Karo

Meeting mein sirf kaam discuss karo — payment WhatsApp par pehle se agree honi chahiye. Ambiguity avoid karo.

5

Kuch Bhi Uncomfortable Lage Toh Leave Karo

Apni gut feeling trust karo. Koi pressure dal raha ho, unsafe feel ho raha ho — politely leave karo aur report karo.

6

Meeting Ka Record Rakho

Agreement WhatsApp message par confirm karo meeting ke baad — oral agreements ke proof nahi hote.
